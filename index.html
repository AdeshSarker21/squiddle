Apply online today to borrow £100 to £1500

Squiddle can help you borrow £100 to £1500, repaid over 4 to 6 months.

Our entire application is 100% online with no upfront or hidden fees. We require a few quick details about yourself and how much you would like to borrow. You will receive an instant decision and subject to some further checks, we can provide funds to your bank account within 1 hour or the same day of applying.

We are proud to be an alternative lender in the UK and we are here to help people borrow money on more flexible terms and affordable rates.

As a direct lender, we will keep your details safe and secure. Applying may impact your credit score.

We are confident that we provide a competitively priced product. You are able to check alternatives.
